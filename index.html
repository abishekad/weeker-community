Introduction

35 Years of Compassion, Commitment, and Community Service:

For over 35 years, our NGO has been dedicated to serving humanity with compassion, integrity, and unwavering commitment. What began as a small initiative driven by a desire to help those in need has grown into a trusted organization making a meaningful difference in countless lives.

Our Journey

Since our founding, we have worked tirelessly to address the challenges faced by vulnerable and underserved communities. Through changing times and growing needs, our mission has remained the same: to uplift, empower, and create sustainable change among the marginalized communities.

Over the past three and a half decades, we have expanded our reach, strengthened our programs, and built strong partnerships with communities, volunteers, donors, and institutions that share our vision of a more just and caring society.

WCDT AIMS

Agricultural Development

To organize, educate, train and Develop Rural small and marginal farmer in villages for social change and development.

Women Empowerment

To enhance the socio economic of rural poor disadvantaged Girls-Women group.

Dalit Education

To provide Dalit remedial education, skill Training and other Training towards reasoning power, bargaining power and empowerment through education.

Youth Promotion

Special concentration on Dalit youths, Dalit women, Dalit children to promote them towards better life through various Training skills and power.

Vocational Training

Provide Vocational and skill Training according to the needs of the situation and better employment opportunities with professional skills.

Social Justice

To eradicate child labour, bonded labour systems, poverty, middlemen role slavery, exploitation, discrimination by birth, sexual exploitation etc.
